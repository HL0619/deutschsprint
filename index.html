Grammar Quick Guide

Grammar Essentials

Tap 🔊 on any example to hear it spoken.

German Articles: der, die, das
▼

Every German noun has a gender — masculine (der), feminine (die), or neuter (das). Always learn the article with the word.

der Kaffee
coffee
die Apotheke
pharmacy
das Brötchen
bread roll
💡 Always say der Kaffee, not just Kaffee.
Polite Requests: ich hätte gerne
▼

"Ich hätte gerne…" = "I would like…" — used in shops, cafés, and restaurants every day.

Ich hätte gerne einen Kaffee.
Ich hätte gerne das Hähnchen.
💡 This works in almost any shop. Learn it first!
Asking Where: Wo ist…?
▼

"Wo ist…?" = "Where is…?" — essential for navigating Germany.

Wo ist die Toilette?
Wo ist der Bahnhof?
Wo ist das Rathaus?
💡 The article stays the same after "Wo ist".
